Booking Your Meetings

Quickly and easily book your office space using our system.

User Management

Each company has a Master account which can manage its users and revoke access to specific accounts at anytime. This will also remove any meetings the revoked account created.

Personnel Assignment

Easily communicate with your team with the ability to assign personnel to a specific booking instance as well as add a small description of what the meeting is about.

Account Notifications

With our custom notification system whenever a user is added or removed from a meeting or the meeting is canceled, they will receive an alert letting them know what happened.

Color Coded Statuses

Tell what a room's status is at a glance with our color coded badges each room receives.
